Produção docente do DCN

Aloísio José Bueno Cotta

I hold a Bachelors degree in Chemistry from the Federal University of Viçosa (2005), a Masters (2007), and a Ph.D. (2010) in Geosciences from UNICAMP. Since 2011, I have been a professor of General, Environmental, Analytical, and Instrumental Chemistry at the Department of Natural Sciences (DCN) at CEUNES/UFES.My expertise includes reference material certification (RM), in-house RM preparation, and quality assurance protocols for chemical assays. I have extensive experience in ICP-MS, AAS, HPLC, and GC methodologies, ensuring compliance with SANTE and ISO guidelines. Additionally, I specialize in FTIR, Zeta Potential, XRD, and XRF analysis, as well as statistical tools for data processing, spatial modeling, and experimental design.I have worked on water quality modeling to evaluate urban organic loads in river systems, considering nutrient cycling (N and P), self-purification capacity, and saline intrusion. My research also includes groundwater contamination assessment at Guriri beach resort, leading to the implementation of chlorination treatment solutions.Other research areas involve oil sample preparation for inorganic constituent analysis, struvite synthesis and characterization, and chromatographic methods (HPLC-DAD, GC-MS) for pesticide determination.
